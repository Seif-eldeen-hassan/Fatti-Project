Born from a passion for baking, Faties has become Egypt's most viral donut brand, captivating the nation and the world. With over 400 million views on social media in just 8 months, Faties has taken TikTok and Instagram by storm, thanks to 1,000 viral videos and posts. In 2023 alone, we sold over 100,000 donuts—all through the power of social media.

The journey began when Faties, at just 14 years old, discovered her love for baking in an unexpected place: the desert. What started as a personal discovery turned into a professional calling, leading her to pursue formal training in a baking and pastry program. She fell in love with the creative possibilities of the culinary world—where no one ever gets bored.

Upon returning to Egypt, Faties followed her dream and launched her own donut brand. Today, Faties Donuts is not just a business but a community of donut lovers, where every creation is crafted with passion, creativity, and a touch of magic.
